How to make a delicious gulab jamuns?

To make delicious gulab jamuns, it is important to gather all the necessary ingredients beforehand. Do not proceed if even one of the ingredients is missing or insufficient.

Ingredients you need

  • Milk powder
  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Baking soda
  • Ghee or unsalted butter
  • Milk (for kneading)
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Lemon juice
  • Oil or ghee (for deep frying)

Step by step guide

  1. Make the sugar syrup
  2. Prepare the dough
  3. Add ghee
  4. Add milk gradually
  5. Divide the dough
  6. Heat the oil
  7. Fry the jamuns
  8. Soak in syrup
